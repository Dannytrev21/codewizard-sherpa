**Refactor decisions:**

- **Models extracted to `_dockerfile_models.py`.** Story §"Notes for the implementer"
  promotes the tagged-union sum type as optional; with the LOC budget the
  cleaner shape is a single `Stage` model carrying every directive's effect
  (vs. ≥ 18 Directive variants). The `_tokenize_dockerfile_line` extract IS
  mandated (Refactor step 1) and lives in `_dockerfile_parse.py`. The probe
  module imports both via a thin re-export surface.
- **Parser as a separate sibling module (`_dockerfile_parse.py`).** Keeps the
  per-module LOC budget in `dockerfile.py` honest; the parser is pure and
  has its own surface area.
- **Validator helper extended via `rglob`, not by moving schemas.** The story
  prescribes `src/codegenie/schema/probes/layer_c/*.schema.json` (layer-scoped
  subdir); the existing validator only globbed the flat `probes/` directory.
  The one-line `glob → rglob` widening is the Open/Closed addition: future
  layer-scoped sub-schemas need zero validator edits. Alternative: flatten the
  schemas under `probes/` to match Phase 0/1 — rejected because the story
  intent is layer-scoped organization and the widening is structurally trivial.
- **No `Directive` tagged-union sum type.** Promoted to "implementer's call"
  by the validator; under the LOC budget a tagged-union with ≥ 18 variant
  models adds ~50 LOC of pure type declarations. The single `Stage` model +
  match-on-string-`kind` in `_dockerfile_parse._update_stage` is well within
  budget and the parser tests pin every directive's effect — mutation
  catches are structural rather than type-system-enforced.
- **`StaticShellEvidence` + `RunCommandEntry` as typed Pydantic models** —
  Design-Patterns harden D1 demand. Both `frozen=True, extra="forbid"`.
- **Slice payload `confidence` field added to DockerfileProbe.** The AC
  prose says "emits `confidence='unavailable'`" — that's a slice-level
  field, not the envelope-level `ProbeOutput.confidence`. Added to the
  slice + sub-schema + AC test fixture.
- **`find_dockerfiles` is repo-wide, not just root-level.** The multi-Dockerfile
  AC requires `apps/api/Dockerfile` to be discovered; `rglob` is the right
  primitive.
